📜 Game Guide

📖 The Story

  • Emoji Land is under siege! Dark forces have corrupted the peaceful emoji inhabitants, turning them into hostile creatures bent on destruction.
  • As the legendary Cowboy Hero, you must defend Emoji Land from endless waves of corrupted emojis.
  • Defeat enemies, collect power-ups, and grow stronger with each battle. Only by mastering your abilities can you push back the darkness!
  • Seek out the mysterious 🧙‍♂️ Wandering Merchant who appears to aid brave heroes with powerful items and knowledge.
  • Every 11 levels, a massive Boss emerges — defeat it to prove your worth and continue your quest!
  • At exactly 15 minutes, a terrifying ☠️ Mega Boss appears — the ultimate challenge! Defeat it to win the game!
  • Your Goal: Survive as long as possible, level up your character, unlock new abilities, and become the ultimate defender of Emoji Land!

🎮 Controls

  • WASD / Arrow Keys — Move your character
  • Mouse — Aim & control fire direction
  • Left Click — Dash (or fire if Click-to-Fire cheat is on)
  • P / Escape — Pause game
  • Insert — Spawn Player 2 (costs 1 life)
  • Mobile — Left stick: move · Right stick: aim · Double-tap right stick: dash
  • Gamepad — Left stick: move · Right stick: aim · RT: dash · Start/B: pause

👥 Player 2 Controls

  • Numpad 8 — Aim/Shoot Up
  • Numpad 5 — Aim/Shoot Down
  • Numpad 4 — Aim/Shoot Left
  • Numpad 6 — Aim/Shoot Right

⚔️ How to Play

  • Survive endless waves of corrupted emoji enemies that grow stronger over time.
  • Shoot enemies to earn XP — fill your XP bar to level up and choose powerful stat upgrades.
  • Collect boxes dropped by enemies to unlock random power-ups that enhance your abilities.
  • Gather 🍎 apples — collect 5 to gain +1 max heart and fully heal yourself.
  • Find the 🧙‍♂️ Merchant who appears randomly to sell power-ups, XP, and healing for coins or apples.
  • Destroy 🛢️ barrels to trigger massive fire explosions that damage nearby enemies.
  • Break 🧱 bricks for strategic positioning and to clear paths.
  • Enemies scale — they get faster, tougher, and more numerous as you level up.
  • Boss battles — every 11 levels, a giant boss version of a random enemy spawns. Defeat it for glory!
  • Earn coins during runs to spend in the Permanent Upgrades shop between games.

💡 Pro Tips & Strategies

  • Master the Dash: Your dash is your lifeline! Use it to escape tight situations, dodge projectiles, and reposition quickly. Time it wisely — it has a cooldown!
  • Upgrade Wisely: Early game, prioritize movement speed and fire rate. Late game, focus on damage and survivability. Adapt to your playstyle!
  • Kite Enemies: Keep moving in circles while shooting. Never let enemies surround you!
  • Synergize Power-Ups: Combine Auto-Aim + Magnetic Shots for homing bullets. Pair Explosive Bullets + V-Shape Shots for massive area damage!
  • Use the Environment: Shoot barrels when enemies are nearby for free damage. Use bricks as temporary shields.
  • Merchant Strategy: Save coins for power-ups early, but buy XP if you're close to leveling up before a boss fight.
  • Apple Management: Don't waste apples at full health! Save them for emergencies or trade to the merchant.
  • Boss Preparation: Try to level up right before levels 11, 22, 33, etc. to face bosses with fresh upgrades.
  • Frozen Enemies: Ice Projectile is amazing for crowd control. Freeze dangerous enemies first!
  • Dash Invincibility: If unlocked, use your dash offensively to phase through enemy projectiles and crowds.

🧙‍♂️ The Wandering Merchant

  • The Merchant appears randomly during your run at a safe location.
  • Buy Power-Ups: Spend coins to purchase specific power-ups instead of relying on random boxes.
  • Buy XP: Trade coins or apples for instant XP to level up faster.
  • Healing: Trade apples or coins to restore hearts.
  • Fire Rate Boost: Temporary fire rate increase for a limited time.
  • Strategy: Visit the merchant when you need a specific power-up or are close to leveling up before a boss!

🏪 Permanent Upgrades Shop

  • Access from the main menu to spend coins earned across all runs.
  • Permanent Stat Boosts: Increase player damage, speed, magnet radius, luck, and more — these persist between games!
  • Unlock Power-Ups: Purchase unlockable power-ups to add them to the drop pool.
  • Unlock Characters: Buy new playable characters with unique abilities.
  • Unlock Maps: Purchase new battlegrounds with different themes and challenges.
  • Quality of Life Upgrades: 4th Heart (3000 🪙) and 4 Level Choices (3500 🪙) — powerful permanent bonuses!
  • Pro Tip: Invest in permanent damage and speed early for easier runs!

🏆 Trophies & Cheats

  • Complete in-game challenges to earn trophies (achievements).
  • Each trophy unlocks a unique cheat you can toggle before starting a run.
  • Cheats include: Double Speed, Giant Mode, Click-to-Fire, No Gun Mode, and more!
  • Access via the Trophies button on the main menu.
  • Warning: Some cheats make the game harder, some easier — experiment and have fun!

🎯 Advanced Tactics

  • Circle Strafing: Move in circles around enemy groups while shooting inward — classic but effective!
  • Corner Camping: Use map corners to limit enemy approach angles, but watch out for being surrounded!
  • Barrel Chains: Line up multiple barrels and shoot one to create a chain explosion.
  • Puddle Zones: Create "kill zones" with Puddle Trail where enemies slow down for easy targeting.
  • Dash Through: With Dash Invincibility, dash through enemy crowds to escape or reposition safely.
  • Boss Kiting: Keep maximum distance from bosses while dealing damage — they're slow but deadly up close!
  • Power-Up Combos: Auto-Aim + Ricochet + Explosive Bullets = screen-clearing chaos!
